Corinne Ivy Squier Lewars

Computer programmer, artist, musician, writer, founder of band Ramjet Harpsichord.

I am a 65-year-old Buffalo, New York trans woman, publisher of several novels and a memoir, a painter of some 20 years standing and a cartoonist for over 30 years. I am currently studying Python, JavaScript, and HTML programming in order to start a new career. I have been a small newspaper editor and photo editor three times, and an art director for the first Buffalo publication devoted to the arts in our city. I have also been a mental health therapy aide, a draftsman and illustrator for an archaeological firm, and the owner and proprietor of my own graphic arts company.

Books and Achievements

  • Published a memoir of my gender transition entitled A Long Way Down From the Roof, avaliable on Kindle Driect Publishing
  • Published a science fiction novel, Woman Out of Place, available on Kindle Direct Publishing.
  • Worked for five years as a draftsman and illustrator for Panamerican Consultants, Inc., an archaeological consulting firm which surveys areas of cultural and archaelogical significance all over the Eastern Seaboard and Puerto Rico.
  • Worked as a content creator for Substack, producing a regular newsletter called Corinne's Chronicle three times a week, which covered topics of social and LGBTQ import as well as political issues.
  • Have performed in more than sixteen bands over the past forty years, as a bass player, lead guitarist, and lead vocalist and songwriter at various times.
